General Hiking Tips

  • Always let someone know your plans and expected return time.
  • Pack essentials: water, snacks, first aid, map, and sunscreen.
  • Stay hydrated and carry more water than you think you'll need.

Seasonal Considerations

  • Avoid midday heat in summer; start early and wear breathable clothing.
  • In winter, dress in layers and check trail conditions for ice or snow.

Gear Recommendations

  • Invest in good hiking boots for comfort and safety.
  • Hiking poles can help with balance and reduce strain on knees.
  • Don't forget sunscreen and a hat to protect against UV rays.

Local Resources

  • Join guided hikes through local outdoor organizations.
  • Visit outdoor shops in Utah Valley for gear and advice.
  • Use shuttle services to access remote trailheads.

Leave No Trace

  • Stay on designated trails to protect vegetation and wildlife.
  • Pack out all trash, including biodegradable items.
